How To Use The Wirecutter

Gadget shopping can be difficult at times. But I think I can help.

How Do I Use Your Site?

Pick the category of gadgets that you want to shop in, and then read the descriptions of our handful of choices. We've gone with high end, low end, and what we believe is just right.

What's the best way to find out when you've updated The Wirecutter?

Browse through the New and Popular sections to see the latest and greatest gadgets.

How do you choose your gadgets?

I study materials put forth by gadget companies. I talk to the best journalists who review gadgets. I take all the data I can find from their work and from around the web. And then I make up my mind. Sometimes I agree, other times I recommend a lower or higher tiered gadget depending on what I think most people will need.

What if the Kind of thing I want Isn't on your site?

Email me and let me know if you need some help figuring out a particular buying problem.
